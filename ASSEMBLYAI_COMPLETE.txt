## SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
=================================================================

Caption Generation Flow:

1. **User clicks "Generate Captions"**
   ↓
2. **Unified Caption Service**
   ↓
3. **Try AssemblyAI (PRIMARY)**
   - Extract audio from video
   - Upload to AssemblyAI cloud
   - Start transcription job
   - Poll for completion
   - Get word-level timestamps
   - Convert to YouTube-style captions
   - Save to database
   ↓
4. **If AssemblyAI fails → Try Whisper**
   ↓
5. **If Whisper fails → Demo (fake)**
